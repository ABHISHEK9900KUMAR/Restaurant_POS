Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built specifically for Indian independent restaurants, dhabas and cloud kitchens.

📱
QR Table Ordering
Customers scan a QR code at their table and order directly from their phone. No app download needed.
👨‍🍳
Live Kitchen Tracker
Orders appear on the admin dashboard in real-time. Accept, prepare, mark ready — with one tap.
🧾
GST-Ready Billing
Print receipts with your GSTIN, SAC code, CGST + SGST breakdown, and sequential invoice numbers.
💵
Cash + UPI Payments
Accept cash with change calculation or UPI with auto-generated QR. Track both in the daily report.
📊
Daily Sales Report
End-of-day PDF with revenue, top items, table-wise sales, GST collected and cash vs UPI split.
🖨
KOT Printing
Kitchen Order Tickets print to any 80mm or 58mm thermal printer. Chef sees item, qty, and special notes.
🌐
Hindi + English
Full bilingual support — switch between Hindi and English instantly. Menu items show in both languages.
✏️
Special Instructions
"Less spicy, no onion." Customers add notes per item. Notes show on the KOT so the chef knows.
📦
Stock Management
Set quantity limits per item. Auto-marks out of stock when depleted. "Only 3 left" badge on menu.
How it works

From table to kitchen
in under 30 seconds.

1
📷
Customer scans QR
A QR code at each table opens the full menu on the customer's phone. No app, no login needed.
2
🛒
Customer places order
They browse, add items, add notes like "less spicy", and place the order. A tracker shows live status.
3
🔔
Kitchen gets it instantly
Order appears on the admin dashboard and KOT prints automatically. No running, no lost slips.
